Market precedents

Two Indian birth-centre models already prove demand for choice-led maternity care.

Hyderabad | The Sanctum

Premium natural birth, backed by continuity and wellness.

2007 Publicly positioned as serving families since launch.
1,500+ Babies reported by the centre.
3,000+ Couples trained through childbirth classes.
  • Natural birth, VBAC, water birth
  • Midwifery model plus OB/Gyn collaboration
  • Wellness layer: Lamaze, yoga, massage, counselling

Kochi | Birthvillage

Standalone midwifery centre built around one-on-one respectful care.

2008 Publicly positioned as a pioneering Indian birth centre.
1,000+ Mothers and babies reported by the centre.
1:1 Continuity, dignity, and informed consent as core claims.
  • Natural birth, water birth, VBAC
  • Birth education, lactation, postpartum support
  • Integrative care language including Ayurveda
